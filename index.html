Just to clarify

This is just a Website to host my School-Presentations I made with reveal.js. Where does that "obsession" for reveal.js come from you ask? My computer science teacher showed us multiple times the "Death by PowerPoint" TED talk by David JP Phillips and then reveal.js to show us how good it is. Carrying those presentations around was never my favourite thing, and when I realized that reveal.js is just html and javascript, I knew I could host it online. Then GitHub pages came to my mind and I tested it and it worked. And that's the story why I host my school presentations here.
